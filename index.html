OVERVIEW

Photography a part of my hobby and I enjoy exploring different photography styles such as nature, street, travel, and candid photography. Photography is not just about taking pictures but it’s about observing details, understanding light, and telling a story in a single frame. Outside of photography, my life is shaped by moments of movement, exploration, and quiet observation. Like playing football, grounds me both physically and mentally. It also teaches me discipline, resilience, teamwork, and the importance of staying present even under pressure. Besides that, travelling is another things I like too. It allows me to step beyond routine and familiarity, opening my eyes to new places, cultures, and perspectives. Wherever I go, my camera becomes a silent companion, capturing fragments of time that feel honest and unposed, a passing expression and a moment that exists only once. Photography, football, and travel are deeply connected in my life together, they teach me balance between action and stillness, speed and patience, planning and spontaneity.

Hello, I’m Muhammad Qoyyum Bin Kamarudin, 21 years old. Study in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). My academic background focuses on programming, record management, archives, and library sciences, which provides me with a structured approach to organizing digital information. My personal interests in photography, football, and traveling. Beyond my academic life, I am a passionate photography enthusiast. I view photography as a tool for storytelling, allowing me to preserve unposed emotions and fleeting moments that define our everyday experiences. My life is balanced by the strategic discipline of football and the cultural explorations of traveling. These passions not only refine my creative vision but also enhance my technical skills in visual composition, photo editing, and modern web design.
